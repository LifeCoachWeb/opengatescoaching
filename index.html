About Open Gates

A welcoming place to take your next step

Open Gates provides professional, respectful coaching for individuals seeking personal growth, greater confidence, healthier patterns, and meaningful progress.

Coaching is available virtually and in person. Meeting details for in-person appointments are shared directly with confirmed clients.

Our Services

Personalized support for your goals

Life Coaching

Harold Gates helps clients identify goals, work through obstacles, strengthen confidence, and create practical steps toward personal growth.

Coaching may include life transitions, motivation, accountability, purpose, relationships, and personal development.

Eating Disorder Coaching

Roxanne Gates provides compassionate coaching support for people navigating concerns related to eating disorders, body image, recovery, and healthy routines.

Coaching is designed to complement, not replace, appropriate medical or mental-health treatment.
